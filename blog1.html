Why Do Websites End in .com ?

July 3, 2021

What is a Domain name?

When we refer to the internet address or name, a domain or domain name is the location of the website. Say for example, the domain name “google.com” points to the IP address “216.58.216.164”.

Generally, it’s easier to remember a name rather than a long string of numbers. Each computer on the internet has an Internet protocol (IP) address.

Since remembering the IP addresses of all of your favourite websites would be nearly impossible, a group of computer scientists created the domain name system to assign a unique name to each numeric IP address.

Why domain name is important for a business?

A short, memorable domain name for a company helps in creating successful web presence.

Let’s say we visit a company’s website and the site is published through a free web hosting site, the website URL will look somewhat like, www.wix.com/nameofyourbusiness.

This generic address might not leave the same impression as a URL like www.yourbusiness.com will. Having our own domain name makes the company look professional.

Free website hosting is good for a hobby project but for something more serious, it is advisable to go with an affordable web hosting plan.

What is .com and how did it become so popular?

.com stands for commercial and it was first introduced together with other top-level domain names such as .edu, .net, .gov, .mil, .org, .int. It was initially intended to represent the commercial intent of the website but nowadays there is no restriction on what kind of entity can use this domain extension.

Even a non-profit organization can use .com tld(top-level domain). It gained popularity because it was introduced so early on that it became the go-to TLD because it was essentially the first out of the gate. People started to come online in the early 90’s and by that time .com had already been established as the standard for what a domain should look like.

It is also been told that it can at times be damn near impossible to find a .com domain that’s available. Sometimes people even snap up domain names because they know other people are going to want them, with no intention of doing anything other than waiting for the highest bidder to come along.

Benefits of having .com as the domain name.

Nowhere is it written that your start-up would fail if you don’t own .com domain. Ask a layman about what does .com mean and we have a fair chance to get the right answer.

Now ask the same person about any other domain name, it is possible that they don’t even recognize what it means, it’s because they haven’t seen or heard it before.

We can not say .com is better than all other tld names, it’s just very common. They are globally recognized and because of its popularity, it has become like a default domain when people go hunting of their dream project.

This can be related with a real-life example, where we don’t actually use the word ‘toothpaste’ while buying toothpaste. We generally go to the shop and say, “bhaiya, ek colgate dena na!”

That being said, domain name is not the only thing that drives visitors to our website. In fact, careful Search Engine Optimization (SEO) is a bigger driving effect than the domain name we choose. Website with .com domains are more likely to be better for SEO, but that is because it has been around fir so long, and there tends to be a search engine bias toward it, but that doesn’t mean that other domains aren’t searchable.
